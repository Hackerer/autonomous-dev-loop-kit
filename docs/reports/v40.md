## Research
- The lightweight committee baseline still applies one generic quality shape to every repo archetype, even though collect-project-data already detects archetype signals.
- A config-level profile model can stay optional while giving later versions a durable place to attach archetype-aware thresholds and committee emphasis.
- The smallest safe first step is schema plus resolution support, not scoring changes.
- Research gate summary: Introduce a lightweight archetype profile model and resolution path before letting scoring or gates depend on it.
